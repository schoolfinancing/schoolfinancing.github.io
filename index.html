School Financing for Salt Lake Community College Students

We believe that everyone deserves fair financing options, and we are here to provide you with the knowledge needed to find them!

Scholarships

Scholarships and tuition waivers are available at SLCC. Look into SLCC sponsered scholarships like the Presidential Scholarship or the Western Undergraduate Exchange Program, but also take a look at private scholarships like Wells Fargo's CollegeSTEPS scholarship or the ACLU Student Activist scholarship.

FAFSA

FAFSA is the Free Application for Federal Student Aid. This is your one stop shop to apply for federally funded grants, loans, and work-study programs that will help finance your education. Your financial aid office can help you through the application process or any questions you may have about FASFA.

Private loans

Many financial institutions will offer private student loans that you can use to pay for your degree(s).Companies like Wells Fargo offer private loans in addition to the scholarships they offer. Other companies, like Discover, offer student loans that offer cash rewards or other incentives for performing well and getting good grades.
